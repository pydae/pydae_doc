Auxiliar equations

\[\begin{split} \begin{eqnarray} v_d &=& V \sin(\delta - \theta) \\ v_q &=& V \cos(\delta - \theta) \\ p_e &=& i_d \left(v_d + R_a i_d\right) + i_q \left(v_q + R_a i_q\right) \\ \omega_s &=& \omega_{coi} \end{eqnarray} \end{split}\]

Dynamic equations

\[\begin{split} \begin{eqnarray} \frac{ d\delta}{dt} &=& \Omega_b \left(\omega - \omega_s \right) - K_{\delta} \delta \\ \frac{ d\omega}{dt} &=& \frac{1}{2H} \left(p_m - p_e - D \left(\omega - \omega_s \right) \right) \\ \frac{ de'_q}{dt} &=& \frac{1}{T'_{d0}} \left(-e'_q - \left(X_d - X'_d\right) i_d + v_f\right) \\ \frac{ de'_d}{dt} &=& \frac{1}{T'_{q0}} \left(-e'_d + \left(X_q - X'_q\right) i_q\right) \end{eqnarray} \end{split}\]

Algebraic equations#

\[\begin{split} \begin{eqnarray} 0 &=& v_q + R_a i_q + X'_d i_d - e'_q \\ 0 &=& v_d + R_a i_d - X'_q i_q - e'_d \\ 0 &=& i_d v_d + i_q v_q - p_g \\ 0 &=& i_d v_q - i_q v_d - q_g \end{eqnarray} \end{split}\]
